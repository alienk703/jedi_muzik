ABOUT

Hi, my name is Justin, aka JEDI. A music producer based out of Los Angeles, California. I'm not from here, I was raised in Virginia through elementary to highschool. In 2005 I visited Cali for 2 weeks. When I went back to VA I dropped out of college, packed my shit up and made the move out to west. I attended MI while interning for Artisans Music where I eventually became the head engineer/In-house producer. Through that experience I was fortunate to work with some of the biggest names in music such as E-40, Bobby Valentino, Talib Kweli, !llmind, Freddie Gibbs, Kurrupt, Alkaholiks, etc. Currently I am working on my own sound by combining elements of hip hop and electronic music together to create something fresh and new. My first song was premiered by DIPLO on his bbc1xtra DIPLO and friends show and has been receiving continuous support by some of the biggest web-blogs on the planet. Please stay tuned for my upcoming releases and to everyone with a dream and that has listened to my music. THANK YOU!!
